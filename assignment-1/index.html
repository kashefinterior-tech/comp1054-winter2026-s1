Young and Beautiful

by Lana Del Rey

Why this song matters to me

This song matters to me because of its connection to The Great Gatsby and the way it captures a sad yet jazzy atmosphere that feels both glamorous and fragile. The music sounds timeless, as if it belongs to another era, while the lyrics express a quiet fear of losing love and value over time. I have often noticed that this song is played at memorials for young people, which gives it an even deeper emotional weight. For me, it represents the beauty of youth, the sadness of its fragility, and the question of whether love remains when everything else fades.

About the artist

Lana Del Rey is an American singer and songwriter known for her cinematic sound and nostalgic aesthetic. Her music often explores themes of love, loss, fame, and identity, drawing inspiration from vintage Americana and classic Hollywood imagery. “Young and Beautiful” reflects her signature style by blending emotional vulnerability with lush orchestration. The song became widely recognized for its role in The Great Gatsby, where it reinforced the film’s themes of glamour, longing, and impermanence.
